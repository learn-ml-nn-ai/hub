# Strategy — the north star

**Goal:** go from math intuition to production AI engineering the slow, honest way —
reimplementing each idea from first principles, in order, learning in public.

**Shape of the journey:**
- One **superset hub** (this repo) holds the map, compass, and next-step.
- Each **child repo** owns one coherent slice of work and is developed in isolation.
- **GitHub** (org `learn-ml-nn-ai`) is the single source of truth for cross-repo work.
- Progress is **measured, not guessed** — `STATUS.md` is generated from real repo state.

**Why a hub instead of one mega-repo:** isolation keeps each slice focused and
independently shippable; the hub keeps the big picture from getting lost.
